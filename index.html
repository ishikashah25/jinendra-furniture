About Us

Founded in 1999, Jinendra Furniture has been dedicated to the art of fine woodworking for over two decades. Our workshop is a place where tradition meets innovation, and every piece is crafted with care and attention to detail.

We specialize in using premium hardwoods such as teak, oak, and walnut, ensuring each creation is both beautiful and durable. Our design philosophy blends timeless elegance with modern functionality, making our furniture a perfect fit for any home.

Our 9,000 sq.ft. premium showroom showcases a wide range of designs, from classic collections to custom bedroom packages. As a manufacturer of exclusive bedroom sets, we take pride in offering 100% guaranteed solid teak wood ("Saag") furniture — trusted for its unmatched quality and longevity.

From custom commissions to classic collections, our story is built on trust, craftsmanship, and a passion for creating spaces that feel like home.
